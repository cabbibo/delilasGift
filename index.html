Summary

From the creator of Enough, Blarp, My Lil' Donut, Warka Flarka Flim Flam, and L U N E, comes a novel, emotional, computational experience. Delila's Gift is a VR Picture book created using Tiltbrush! It tells the story of a small sea creature named Delila and her struggle to understand belonging, ownership, fear, othering, and the darkness surrounding her.

By using Tiltbrush and using these paintings to create a particle simulation, Delila's Gift lets the users literally *step* into the paintings, playing and pulling on them in an inherently physical fashion.
